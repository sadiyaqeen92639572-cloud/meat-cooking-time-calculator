What Temperature to Cook Chicken in the Oven

Roast a whole chicken at 180°C fan (200°C conventional / 400°F) — about 20 minutes per 500 g plus 20 minutes. Chicken pieces and breasts do best a little hotter at 200°C fan. Whatever the temperature, cook to 74°C internal.
